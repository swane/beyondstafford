Helen Weir, Speech and Language Therapist – CV

DOB: 20th September 1978

 

Personal Profile

Since graduating, I have enjoyed working full time as a Speech and Language Therapist in South Staffordshire. I have worked with adults with acquired communication and swallowing difficulties, in both in-patient and out-patient settings. I have also worked with adults with learning disabilities in a variety of settings. I particularly enjoy working with adults with PMLD using intensive interaction techniques. I have delivered training courses in basic signing, intensive interaction, dysphagia and communication skills to nurses, day centre staff and in residential and nursing homes.
