World Wide Volunteering http://www.wwv.org.uk

(The £10 enables you to use the WWV database three separate times for the duration of a month. When you have paid £10 you are invited to fill in details about the type of volunteer work you are interested in, where you would like to volunteer, the timespan and the cost you can afford. The database then filters to find the organisations which match your requirements showing you the names and profiles of these organisations on the first page, and then a further page of copious details about that organisation and its projects.While you are using the database you can use the back button to change your interests, countries etc as many times as you wish, and each time the program will process your choices. This is all only counted as one of your three visits for £10.)
